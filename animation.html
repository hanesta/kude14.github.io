Animation movies

An animation movie is a type of film that is created using computer-generated imagery (CGI), traditional hand-drawn animation, or a combination of both techniques. These movies typically feature colorful and imaginative visuals, along with engaging characters and fantastical worlds. Animation movies can be targeted towards both children and adults, and they often convey important messages or themes through their storytelling. Some popular animation studios include Disney, Pixar, DreamWorks, and Studio Ghibli. Animation movies have the ability to transport viewers to different worlds and evoke a wide range of emotions, making them a beloved genre in the film industry.
